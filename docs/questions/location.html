Q: Location¶

The Question¶

Please choose your country of residence. |v| Dropdown of Countries |

Which word best describes the area where you live and work?
() Rural
() Suburban
() Urban

Rationale¶

Ask this question if you want to know the location of your community members. The second question may be used to understand the general pattern of geographical environment of your community members.

Full list of responses¶

A list of countries is a political matter, toward an inclusive list, for example, we recommend [ISO_3166-1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1) as a list of countries, as found in plain text[here](https://github.com/umpirsky/country-list/blob/master/data/en/country.txt).

Thoughts for including¶

In the scenario where the question about country of residence is mandatory, we recommend including "Decline to state" option in case respondents do not wish to disclose the information.
